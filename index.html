What this reads & stores

Everything runs in your browser. This page reads up to two files that you pick from your own machine:

  • CollectionState.json: your cards, variants, splits, boosters, decks, and collection level.
  • ProfileState.json (optional): your handle, win/loss record, snap count, ladder rank, wallet balances, and per-card cube totals.

From those files it keeps only what you see on this page. It never reads account IDs, email or login info, auth tokens, or the hidden per-card instance IDs, even though those sit in the same files.

Nothing is uploaded. There is no server, no account, no analytics, no tracking. Your data is saved only in this browser's local storage, and Reset erases it. The card list ships with this site. Card art loads from MarvelSnapZone: those requests carry no collection data, but their server sees your IP and which art your browser asks for, and that reflects which cards you own.
